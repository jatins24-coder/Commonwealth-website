History & Origin

  • First held in 1930 in Hamilton, Canada.
  • Founded as the British Empire Games.
  • Now held every four years, involving 56 nations.
  • Focuses on promoting unity and sportsmanship.

Purpose of Commonwealth Games

Promotes peace and cooperation among nations.
Encourages athletic excellence and fair play.
Supports developing nations in sport.

Commonwealth vs Olympic Games

  • Participants: Commonwealth: 56 nations; Olympics: 206 nations
  • Focus: Commonwealth includes para-sports in main event
  • Governing Body: CGF vs IOC
  • Languages & Culture: Commonwealth focuses on shared heritage
